Overview

She Drive is a women-focused transportation and delivery platform designed to provide safe, comfortable, and reliable services exclusively for women passengers and women drivers. The platform also allows women to send packages securely to other women, ensuring privacy, trust, and safety.

Problem

Many women feel unsafe using traditional ride-hailing services, especially at night or in unfamiliar areas. Additionally, sending personal packages through standard courier services can raise concerns about privacy and trust. There is a need for a safe, female-exclusive solution for both transportation and small-scale delivery

Solution

She Drive offers two core services:

1. Women-to-Women Rides – Female drivers providing safe rides exclusively for women passengers.

2.Women-to-Women Deliveries – Women can send packages to other women with trusted female couriers.

Design System

  • Color Palette:
    High contrast between bright accents (#FF4081, #FFBC0C) and softer tones (#FFF1F5, #8D6E71) improves readability.
    Consistent mood — the warm pinks and amber create a friendly, inviting emotional tone, while white adds clarity and mauve grounds the design.
    Emotionally engaging — vibrant pinks express energy and empowerment, softer blush tones add comfort, and amber suggests optimism.
  • complementary friendly, energetic, and approachable complementary palette that balances warmth and trust. It’s especially suitable for a women-to-women service because it mixes empowerment (Hot pink), energy (yellow), and comfort (soft blush + white) while keeping a professional tone with dusty mauve.

  • Typography:
    Font Family: Montserrat (Google Fonts)
    Font Colors: Black (#000000), Dusty Mauve #8D6E71

  • Icons & Imagery: Minimal line-style icon.

Impact

Enhanced safety and trust for women passengers and senders.

Empowerment and income opportunities for women drivers..

Increased comfort and confidence in ride-hailing and package delivery..


What I Learned

Trust is the true feature — safety-first branding and functionality aren’t “extras”; they’re core to adoption.

Designing for women’s unique needs requires empathy + active listening to uncover pain points beyond the obvious.

Usability testing early prevents major redesign costs later.

A clear, intuitive information architecture directly impacts how safe and confident users feel in-app

Future Improvements

Expand to new cities & rural areas to increase accessibility for women with limited transport options.

Introduce subscription plans for frequent riders and package senders to make services more affordable.

AI-based safety alerts that monitor unusual route changes and automatically notify emergency contacts.
